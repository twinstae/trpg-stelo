Arms & Armor

Armor
◆ Light/Medium/Heavy Armor: 3 Uses, 1/2/3 Cost, Clumsy if
STR below 1/2/3. You may mark a use to ignore up to 1/2/3
damage from an attack.

◆ Shield: 1 Use, 1 Cost. You may mark a use to ignore all damage

from an attack.
